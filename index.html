One Punch-Man Timeline

A quick timeline of before the present series

Heres a timeline beginning at Saitama's Birth

  • 25 years prior - Saitama is born.
  • Approx. 20 Years Prior - Boros and the Dark Matter Thieves begin their journey to planet Earth after receiving a prophecy stating that they will find a worthy opponent for Boros there.
  • 19 Years Prior - Genos is born.
  • Approx. 13 Years Prior - Saitama enters Middle School and is beaten by Piggy Bancon. Mumen Rider attends the same school and is beaten by some delinquents.
  • Approx. 11 Years Prior - Gouketsu wins the first Super Fight tournament.
  • Approx. 10 Years Prior - Zombieman escapes from the House of Evolution
  • At most 7 years ago - Gouketsu fights Orochi and loses, then eats a monster cell.
  • Approx. 4 Years Prior - Genos' town and family are destroyed by a mad cyborg. Dr. Kuseno saves Genos, and transforms him into a cyborg.
  • Approx. 3 Years Prior - Saitama saves a young child from Crablante which began his childhood goal of becoming a hero, and begins his training. Sometime after the event, Agoni founded the Hero Association.
  • Approx. 2.5 Years Prior - The S-Class is created.
  • Approx. 2 Years Prior - Elder Centipede is almost defeated by Blast.
  • Saitama's 300th day of training - Saitama defeats Himawari, and later Personification Of A Light Pull Cord.
  • More than 1.5 years prior - Saitama defeats Octopus Claw Man and saves King
  • More than 1.5 years prior - Saitama's hair begins to fall out. 170,000-Year-Old Cicada Larva attacks Z-City, but is killed by Saitama.
  • More than 1 year prior - Metal Bat is promoted to S-Class, and receives his signature metal bat.
  • Less than One Year Prior - Garou beats up and incapacitates his fellow disciples and is promptly expelled from Bang's dojo.
  • Six Months Prior - Garou steals the identity of Wolfman, and uses his identity to win the Super Fight Tournament. His victory earns him the attention of Suiryu, a famous martial artist and previous 4-time winner of Super Fight. Amai Mask tops the popularity ranking. Stinger joins the hero association.
  • Less than One month Prior - Homeless Emperor obtains his powers from God.
  • Unknown time ago - Flashy Flash caused the 44th graduation class incident, and Speed-o'-Sound Sonic was the sole survivor.
